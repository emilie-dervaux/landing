Startup for clothes made from cigarette butts

Who are we?

We turn cigarette butts into sustainable and stylish clothing.

Every year, billions of cigarette butts are thrown away in nature, polluting the environment and endangering wildlife. We decided to take the bull by the horns by creating a startup that transforms these cigarette butts into high quality clothing. We believe that fashion can be both stylish and environmentally friendly

Our story, mission and products

We are a team of fashion and environmental enthusiasts, brought together by our belief that the two can co-exist in harmony. We started by collecting cigarette butts in our town, before realising that we could do much more with them than just throw them in the bin. After months of research and development, we came up with an innovative technique to turn cigarette butts into a durable and resistant textile material.

Our mission is to raise awareness about cigarette butt pollution, while offering sustainable and stylish alternatives to traditional fashion. We believe that fashion can be a force for good, using sustainable materials and encouraging ethical and environmentally friendly practices.

We offer a range of men's and women's clothing made from our exclusive textile material. Our collection includes t-shirts, shirts, jackets and trousers, all made with the same attention to detail and quality as the leading fashion brands
